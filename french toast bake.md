# French Toast Bake

## Serves ?

## What you'll need
1. Baking Dish
2. 2 bread slice / person
3. 2 egg / person
4. Tb spoon brown sugar / person

## What to do
1. Spray dish.
2. Shred bread.
3. Scramble eggs w brown sugar.
4. Bake at 350 for 15-20 mins (until eggs are solid).

## How to serve
Just eat it.
